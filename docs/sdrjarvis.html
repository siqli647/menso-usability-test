Cross-Persona Takeaways

Lead fit is the first trust gate.

Each persona judged value by whether visible leads matched the promised ICP. Any mismatch made the automation feel risky before email quality could be evaluated.

Users need audit trails, not just queues.

Approval history, lead rationale, and generated copy need click-through detail views so users can understand why the AI made each decision.

Dry-run evidence needs a business bridge.

Founders and operators need previewable drafts, deliverability expectations, and campaign-level analytics before trusting the tool with real outbound.
