Cross-Persona Takeaways

Trust breaks at evidence inspection.

Users can find campaigns, leads, approvals, and analytics, but they cannot easily audit why leads were selected or inspect draft quality from history.

Status clarity is the conversion moment.

Dry run, running, sent, pending, and reply states need a clearer story before users will treat results as real outbound signal.

Expansion depends on workspaces and reporting.

Consultants and growth hires need client/workspace separation, pricing commitments, and exportable analytics before the product can replace a stack.
